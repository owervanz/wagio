How it works

Three steps. Sixty seconds.

Just type in Telegram: "I finished a $500 website for Acme Corp." The AI understands and creates your invoice instantly.

2

Share the QR code or payment link. Client pays in USDC using any Solana wallet. Settlement confirms in ~2 seconds.
